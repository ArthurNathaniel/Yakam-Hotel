Bono Manso located in the Bono East region, situated seventeen kilometers Northeast of Techiman (capital of ancient bono kingdom) and now situated fifty kilometers from Nkoransa district. This town dates back in history as one of the prominent cities in the history of the trans-Atlantic slave trade.
