Conference Hall

The Yakam Hotel Conference Room is the perfect venue for business meetings, conferences, and corporate events. With a spacious layout and modern amenities, our conference room is designed to meet the needs of businesses of all sizes.
The conference room can comfortably accommodate up to 100 guests and is equipped with state-of-the-art audiovisual equipment, including a projector, sound system, and high-speed internet. Our professional staff is on hand to assist with any technical requirements, ensuring that your event runs smoothly and seamlessly.
In addition to its modern amenities, the Yakam Hotel Conference Room boasts stunning views of the surrounding cityscape, providing a picturesque backdrop for any event. Whether you are hosting a large conference or an intimate business meeting, our conference room is the ideal venue to impress your clients and colleagues.
Contact us today to book the Yakam Hotel Conference Room for your next event and take advantage of our tailored packages designed to meet your specific requirements.
