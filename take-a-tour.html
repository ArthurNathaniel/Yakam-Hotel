FULLER FALLS

Fuller Falls serves tourists with a serene environment for relaxation and a waterfall which gives a strikingly picturesque appearance. BOABENG FIEMA

BOABENG FIEMA

Boabeng-Fiema Monkey Sanctuary has its roots based on African Tradition, culture and religion aimed at brotherhood, sincerity from man to animal. The native African has a strong belief in his gods. However, European Christians introduced Christianity into Africa, and converted many Africans to Christianity. Some Africans however, decided to stick to their tradition and culture.

BONO MANSO SLAVE MARKETS

BONO MANSO SLAVE MARKETS

Bono Manso located in the Bono East region, situated seventeen kilometers Northeast of Techiman (capital of ancient bono kingdom) and now situated fifty kilometers from Nkoransa district. This town dates back in history as one of the prominent cities in the history of the trans-Atlantic slave trade.

KINTAMPO WATERFALLS

KINTAMPO WATERFALLS

It is just after the Falls Rest Stop when moving Northwards, on the right side of the road. This waterfall, one of the main natural attractions in the area, is hidden in the forest and it is formed by three main drops where the longest drop measures 25 metres (82 ft) in height, and, after a number of steps and cascades, the river falls about 70 metres(230ft).
